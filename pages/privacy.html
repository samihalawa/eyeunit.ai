Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: February 21, 2024

1. Introduction

EyeUnit.ai ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Professional credentials and affiliations
  • Email address and phone number
  • Organization or institution details

Medical Information

  • Retinal images and scans
  • Diagnostic history
  • Treatment records
  • Healthcare provider information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve our AI models and diagnostic capabilities
  • To conduct research and development
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To process your requests and transactions

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

End-to-end Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

HIPAA Compliance

We adhere to strict healthcare data protection standards.

Access Controls

Strict access controls and authentication measures.

Regular Audits

Continuous security monitoring and regular audits.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare Providers

    When necessary for your medical care and treatment.

  • Research Partners

    Only anonymized data for research purposes.

  • Service Providers

    Who assist in operating our services and infrastructure.

  • Legal Requirements

    When required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of your personal information.

Request Corrections

Update or correct inaccurate information.

Request Deletion

Ask us to delete your personal data.

Opt-out

Choose not to participate in research.
